How Does It Work?

The Fake News Detector uses advanced machine learning techniques to analyze news articles and determine whether they are likely to be real or fake.

Here's a simple overview of how it works:

  • 1. You submit the news article: When you paste a news article into the text box, the model processes it.
  • 2. Text analysis: The model breaks down the article into patterns of words and phrases it has learned from thousands of articles.
  • 3. Prediction: Using its training, the model calculates the probability of whether the news is likely to be real or fake.
  • 4. Result: You see the prediction in percentage terms of how likely the article is real or fake.
